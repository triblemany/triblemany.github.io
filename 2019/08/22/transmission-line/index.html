RLCG to $\gamma$ Z
$$
\gamma=\sqrt{(R+j\omega L)(G+j\omega C)}=\alpha+j\beta
$$

$$
Z=\sqrt{\frac{R+j\omega L}{G+j\omega C}}
$$

$\gamma$ Z to RLCG
$$
R={\rm Re}{\gamma Z}
$$

$$
L={\rm Im}{\gamma Z}/\omega
$$

$$
G={\rm Re}{\gamma/Z}
$$

$$
C={\rm Im}{\gamma/Z}/\omega
$$
